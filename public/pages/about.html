What we do

We are building Africa's first innovation learning Platform, needed for rapid ideation, idea data-collection, e-learning, and investment in ideas.

Why we do it

There is a growing mismatch of labour supply and demand that exists in the entrepreneurial ecosystem. Despite having a high youth population of 60%, Africa still has the lowest average rate of innovation in the world.

Slow economic growth, inadequate education, limited idea-visibility, and geographical location are stopping countries from meeting their innovation goals.
