Our Services

We research and ideate to provide content for you in the areas of planning, development, management & social impact.

Idea Architecture
Idea architecture is a neccessary first step for every idea. we provide the content to flesh out your idea, we plan out the objectives, goals, resources & tools that your idea will need for each stage of its development. our tools include:

1.Idea breakdown: Market research & SWOT analysis.

2.Idea starter kit: TO-DO list, resources & tools.

3.Idea review of your existing information.

Design thinking content
We work with you as consultants or within your organization as intrapreneurs to design-think on your problem space and propose feasible solutions. Our tools include:

1.Problem space analysis.

2.Idea consolidation & prioritization analysis.

Idea content specification
We organize your ideas into content that helps you better communicate and pitch your vision to the public. our tools include:

1.Creative writing for: Blogs, books, articles, animation storyboards.

2.App wireframes & user flow specification.

3.Business plan writting & presentation content.

4.Website content.

5.Social media Content calender.

Business development consultant
We help startups & existing businesses develop and meet their idea or business objectives. We brainstorm on their business model, product type or service delivery method to find the content that works best for their idea or business.
Branding & interaction design
Together with our clients, we work on how their ideas for a business, product & service will be represented. By understanding their purpose, passion, mission, vision, and what problem space they intend to explore; we help create a unique brand experience that will meet their objectives. our tools include:

1.Brand guideline proposal.

Idea marketing & management services
We help structure a sustainable plan for your business, project, physical product & app idea's adoption into the market. We also access the success of your existing marketing efforts & propose relevant alternatives to improve & manage your performance. our tools include:

1.Idea marketing content: Go-to-market strategy, product & service roadmap, buyers journey structure.

2.Idea marketing re-evaluation.

Social impact development & management
As social entrepreneurs We ideate & propose social impact ideas that match ur personal or business ethos to ensure a positive & sustainable social footprint for you and your business. our tools include:

1.Personal social impact Project/initiative proposal

2.Corporate social impact Project/initiative proposal
